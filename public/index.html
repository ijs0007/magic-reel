How It Works

1 · Add who gets it. Enter each person's name and email at the top, and set how much footage each one can pull.

2 · Drop your film. Drag the video onto the drop zone at the bottom (or click to choose it).

3 · Walk away. Magic Reel uploads, prepares a private preview, and emails everyone their own link — you can close the tab once it says it's done.

They get a private page to scrub the footage, mark the parts they want, and download a clean cut — capped at the limit you set.

Part of the Magic Suite. Reel is one of four connected apps that share your login and projects: Story Maker (write the script, build cast & crew), Reel (this app — share footage for selects), Marquee (thumbnails & YouTube upload), and Credits (end-credit roll). Switch between them from the links up top.
